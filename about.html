About Theodore Gercken!


Theodore Gercken is the developer of LibertyBot, a high-school student at The College Preparatory School (California), and an avid Libertarian activist. He is currently the Secretary and Communications Director for the Libertarian Party of Alameda County, the Vice-Chair for the Libertarian Youth Caucus, and the Communications Director and a Strategic Consultant for several Libertarian campaigns. In the past he has been on and led state-level committees such as the California Platform Committee, of which he was the Secretary in the 2021-2022 session.
